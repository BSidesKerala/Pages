About Security BSides Kerala

BSides Kerala is all about building a vibrant and open cyber security community where learning, sharing, and connecting come first. Our conference is packed with technical content and fresh ideas, designed for anyone passionate about staying ahead in the fast-changing world of security.

From industry veterans to students, we bring together people eager to explore emerging trends and tackle real-world challenges. Behind the scenes, we are a team of seasoned cyber security professionals and hackers with years of experience, committed to creating an event that is engaging, inclusive, and truly impactful for everyone involved.
