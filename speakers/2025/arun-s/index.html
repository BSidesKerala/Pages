Principal Security Architect at IBM




Panel discussion: Building Resilient Software, The Intersection of Product Security, DevSecOps, and AppSec

In today's product development phases, security must be woven into every stage of the software lifecycle. This panel will explore the critical intersection of Product Security, DevSecOps, and Application Security (AppSec) - shedding light on best practices, emerging challenges, and innovative strategies to build resilient, secure, and scalable software. Join industry experts as they discuss how to seamlessly integrate security into development workflows, balance agility with risk management, and future-proof software against evolving threats.
