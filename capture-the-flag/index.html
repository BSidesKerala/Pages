BESEC.CTF
CTF Competition

Capture-the-flag competition powered by Beagle Security.



Are you ready to test your skills in a cybersecurity competition like no other? besec.ctf, organized by Beagle Security, is set to challenge and inspire the brightest minds in ethical hacking at BSides Kerala 2025.

Taking place on February 7th and 8th, 2025, at the Jain University Campus in Kochi, besec.ctf promises an intense, hands-on experience designed to push your expertise to its limits. Participants will face a wide range of advanced challenges, including network exploitation, cryptographic puzzles, reverse engineering, and coding tasks. This is a unique opportunity to engage with real-world cybersecurity scenarios that demand precision, quick thinking, and innovative problem-solving..

The stakes are high, and the rewards are even higher. Top-performing teams will earn recognition and incredible prizes, solidifying their place as leaders in the cybersecurity field. Whether you’re looking to showcase your talent, learn from cutting-edge challenges, or simply experience the camaraderie of a world-class event, besec.ctf has something for everyone.

Mark your calendar for February 7th and 8th, 2025, and join us at Jain University Campus in Kochi for this unforgettable experience. Don’t miss your chance to capture the flag and make your mark in the future of cybersecurity competitions. Register now to secure your spot for besec.ctf at BSides Kerala 2025!

F.A.Q

  • Do I need to register for BSides Kerala to participate in besec.ctf?

    Yes, to participate in besec.ctf, you must first register for BSides Kerala, followed by completing your registration on the besec.ctf platform.

  • Is besec.ctf an online or offline event?

    besec.ctf is an offline event, and all team members must be physically present at the CTF venue to participate.

  • Can any of my team members participate online in besec.ctf?

    No, all team members must be present at the venue to participate in besec.ctf.

  • Will besec.ctf be live on the night of the first day?

    Yes, the CTF will be live on the night of the first day.

  • What should I bring to participate in besec.ctf?

    You should bring a laptop with the necessary tools installed and ensure you have access to a stable internet connection.

  • Will internet access be provided at the besec.ctf venue?

    No, participants are required to bring their own internet connection as a prerequisite for the event.

  • Can my team join besec.ctf with spot registration?

    Yes, spot registrations are allowed for teams wishing to join besec.ctf.

  • Can I join besec.ctf after it has already started?

    Yes, you can join after the CTF begins, but extra time will not be allotted to solve the challenges.

  • Can an individual participate in besec.ctf as a team?

    Yes, an individual can register and participate as a single-person team in besec.ctf.

  • How can I seek help from the organizers during besec.ctf?

    You can ask questions directly to the organizers present at the event regarding the CTF.
