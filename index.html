Keynote Speakers

Here are our keynote speakers!



Manoj Abraham IPS, ADGP (L&O)

Manoj Abraham IPS is currently the Additional Director General of Police, Thiruvanthapuram and the Head of Cyber Security, in Kerala State. He has won numerous national and international awards in areas of cyber security, social policing and traffic schemes.
He is the architect of Kerala police Cyberdome - a PPP initiative in Cyber security and also the Man behind c0c0n - International Cyber Security Conference.

BSides Kerala Logo Reveal

The BSides Kerala was announced and artwork unveiled by Mr. Susanth Kurunthil, CEO of Infoparks Kerala, and Dr. Letha Janaki, Pro Vice Chancellor of Jain (Deemed-to-be University), Kochi, on 29 October 2024, celebrating Kerala’s rich cultural heritage.
Also in attendance was Mr. Balakrishnan A, Executive Director Geojit Financial services and Mr. Abhijith B R.
