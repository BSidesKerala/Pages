BSides Kerala 2025 Speakers

Biju K

Head of Fraud Risk Management at Federal Bank

Panel discussion: The SaaS ASM Conundrum, Managing Exposure in a 2025 Financial World

As organizations continue to embrace Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solutions for greater scalability and flexibility, managing financial and operational exposure has become increasingly complex. In 2025, the financial world faces new challenges as businesses navigate the evolving landscape of cloud-based tools, vendor relationships, and compliance requirements. This panel will explore the risks and opportunities of SaaS adoption, focusing on the financial implications of shifting from traditional software models to cloud-based platforms.

Biju K

Biju K is the Head of Operational Risk and Information Security at Federal Bank, with extensive experience in managing risk and security frameworks in the banking sector.

With certifications including CISA, CRISC, DCPLA, and ISO 27701LA, Biju has worked with Federal Bank for over 16 years, leading fraud risk management, operational risk, and information security initiatives. His expertise spans banking, customer relationship management (CRM), project management, and the implementation of advanced security solutions.
