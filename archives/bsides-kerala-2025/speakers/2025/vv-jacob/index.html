BSides Kerala 2025 Speakers

V V Jacob

Senior General Manager, Systems at Malayala Manorama Co. Pvt. Ltd.


Panel discussion: Building, Nurturing, and Retaining Cyber Security talent in the Gen AI Era: Challenges and Opportunities

As generative AI reshapes the cybersecurity landscape, the demand for skilled professionals has never been higher. This panel will explore the challenges and opportunities in building, nurturing, and retaining cybersecurity talent in an era where AI technologies are becoming integral to defense strategies. Experts will discuss the evolving skill sets required to tackle AI-driven threats, the importance of upskilling the workforce, and strategies to attract and retain top talent in a competitive market. The conversation will also cover how organizations can foster inclusive environments, promote career growth, and create pathways for long-term engagement in the cybersecurity field.

V V Jacob

V V Jacob is a Senior General Manager at Malayala Manorama Co. Pvt. Ltd. With over three decades of experience in the media production industry, he is a seasoned expert in IT strategy, system administration, and business development. Jacob has been instrumental in transforming the IT landscape at Malayala Manorama through cutting-edge technology implementations, including storage solutions and IT infrastructure upgrades.

Jacob’s leadership led to the successful implementation of Hitachi Vantara's Virtual Storage Platform and Hitachi Advanced Servers, greatly enhancing the company’s IT performance, scalability, and reliability. Under his guidance, Malayala Manorama achieved faster data processing, reduced operational costs, and optimized power and cooling efficiency.

BSides Kerala is all about building a vibrant cyber security community where learning, sharing, and connecting comes first.
