BSides Kerala 2025 Speakers

Deepu S

Chief Information Security Officer at Muthoot Microfin Ltd.

Panel discussion: The SaaS ASM Conundrum, Managing Exposure in a 2025 Financial World

As organizations continue to embrace Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solutions for greater scalability and flexibility, managing financial and operational exposure has become increasingly complex. In 2025, the financial world faces new challenges as businesses navigate the evolving landscape of cloud-based tools, vendor relationships, and compliance requirements. This panel will explore the risks and opportunities of SaaS adoption, focusing on the financial implications of shifting from traditional software models to cloud-based platforms.

Deepu S

Deepu S is an experienced cybersecurity professional specializing in BFSI security, governance, and audit. As the Chief Information Security Officer at Muthoot Microfin Ltd., he is responsible for developing and implementing cybersecurity strategies to protect financial data and operations.

His expertise spans cyber strategy, SOC operations, vendor management, network security, and information security governance. He holds a Bachelor of Engineering from Anna University and a Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) certification from ISACA.

BSides Kerala is all about building a vibrant cybersecurity community where learning, sharing, and connecting comes first.
