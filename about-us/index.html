About BSides Kerala

Security BSides Kerala 2024

BSides Kerala is all about building a vibrant cybersecurity community where learning, sharing, and connecting come first. Our conference is packed with technical content and fresh ideas, designed for anyone passionate about staying ahead in the fast-changing world of security.
From industry veterans to students, we bring together people eager to explore emerging trends and tackle real-world challenges.

Behind the scenes, we’re a team of seasoned cyber security professionals with years of experience, committed to creating an event that’s engaging, inclusive, and truly impactful for everyone involved.
