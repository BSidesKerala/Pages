Panel discussion: AI, the Future of Cyber Security and the Evolution of Cyber Warfare

Explore how artificial intelligence is reshaping the future of cyber security and transforming the landscape of cyber warfare. This panel will delve into AI-driven advancements in threat detection, response, and defense strategies, while examining the ethical challenges and evolving tactics of cyber adversaries in an increasingly AI-powered world.
Join industry leaders as they discuss the opportunities, risks, and implications of AI, future of cyber security and the evolution of cyber warfare.

Colonel. Nidhish Bhatnagar (Retd.)

Colonel Nidhish Bhatnagar is an accomplished innovator, thought leader, and policy shaper with over three decades of multifaceted experience. A decorated veteran of the Indian Army, he served for 25 years, earning recognition for his strategic vision and leadership. Subsequently, he transitioned seamlessly into corporate, government, and academic roles, including leading defense programs at Larsen & Toubro and serving as Chief of Staff in key Indian ministries.
At Rashtriya Raksha University, he directed tech-focused schools and spearheaded its startup and innovation incubators, fostering advancements in cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and emerging technologies. His contributions towards enhancing India’s cyber resilience towards conducting cutting edge cyber exercises and training, as also developing cyber products including India’s first indigenous large scale cyber range are noteworthy.

An alumnus of IIT Delhi and the Defence Services Staff College, Colonel Bhatnagar is a sought-after advisor in technology, entrepreneurship, innovation, and training. Currently, he leads initiatives at the Abhayada Consultancy Services, a cybersecurity and management consultancy, driving impactful strategies in critical and emerging tech domains. His expertise continues to shape India’s technological and security landscape.
