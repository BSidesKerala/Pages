BSides Kerala 2025 Speakers

Aadesh Shinde

Threat Researcher at BforeAI




Workshop: Hook, Hack, & TI: Breaking Down Phishing Scams

This workshop delves into the intricacies of phishing, a prevalent cyber threat. Participants will explore various aspects of phishing sites and phishing kits, learning how to investigate and dismantle these threats effectively. The session will cover comprehensive detection rules, techniques, and tools essential for phishing analysis. Attendees will gain hands-on experience in identifying phishing campaigns, utilizing practical tools and methodologies presented during the workshop.

By the end of this interactive session, participants will be equipped with the skills to hunt down phishing sites, understand the mechanics behind phishing kits, and implement robust threat intelligence practices to safeguard against phishing attacks.

Aadesh Shinde

Aadesh Shinde is a Threat Researcher at BforeAI, contributing to the forefront of predictive cybersecurity. With a passion for innovation, Aadesh has an impressive track record, having published over four patents in the field of cybersecurity. His expertise lies in analyzing emerging threats and crafting proactive solutions to secure the digital landscape.

Beyond his technical achievements, Aadesh is an advocate for knowledge sharing and collaboration in the cybersecurity community. He brings a unique perspective shaped by his experience in research and development, ensuring organizations stay ahead of evolving cyber challenges. Aadesh is an engaging speaker, blending technical depth with accessible insights, and has a proven ability to connect with diverse audiences at conferences and seminars.

BSides Kerala is all about building a vibrant cybersecurity community where learning, sharing, and connecting comes first.
