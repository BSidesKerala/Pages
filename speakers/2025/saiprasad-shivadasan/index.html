BSides Kerala 2025 Speakers

Saiprasad Sivadasan

Senior Vice President - Group Cloud Head at Manappuram

Panel discussion: Cloud Security in the era of Artificial Intelligence, Emerging Challenges and Threats

As organizations increasingly integrate Artificial Intelligence (AI) into cloud environments, the security landscape is rapidly evolving. While AI enhances automation, threat detection, and incident response, it also introduces new risks such as adversarial AI, data poisoning, and AI-powered cyberattacks. This panel discussion will explore the emerging security challenges posed by AI in cloud computing, including the complexities of securing large-scale AI models, ensuring data privacy, and mitigating AI-driven threats. Experts from cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, and AI research will share insights on best practices, regulatory implications, and proactive defense strategies. Join us to gain a deeper understanding of how to navigate cloud security in this AI-driven era.

Saiprasad Sivadasan

With over 23 years of experience, Saiprasad Sivadasan is an accomplished Senior Vice President and Group Cloud Head at Manappuram. He is highly skilled in cloud migration, digital transformation, automation, and leveraging platforms like Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) and Azure for business optimization.

Saiprasad has successfully led multiple cloud migration initiatives, transforming financial operations through strategic transitions to Oracle and Adani clouds, optimizing IT infrastructure, and reducing operational costs. His leadership spans industries such as BFSI, retail, healthcare, insurance, and education.

Prior to his current role, Saiprasad served as the CTO at Manappuram, overseeing IT operations across ten companies and driving digital transformations that led to significant improvements in revenue and operational efficiency.
