BSides Kerala 2025 Speakers

Sony A

Chief Information Officer at South Indian Bank




Panel discussion: DPDP Act and Banking: A new era of personal data protection

The Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act is set to redefine how the banking sector handles personal data, ushering in a new era of security and consumer trust. This panel will dive into how the Act is revolutionizing data protection practices, highlighting the challenges and innovations banks face as they adapt to this sweeping regulation. With the rapid rise of digital banking and data-driven technologies, ensuring consumer privacy is more critical than ever. Experts will explore the transformative impact of the DPDP Act on data management, compliance strategies, and the evolving risks of cyber threats. This conversation will also examine how banks can stay ahead of regulatory demands while continuing to innovate. Discover how the DPDP Act is not just a regulatory challenge, but a unique opportunity to build a more secure and transparent financial ecosystem.

Sony A

Sony A is a highly experienced individual currently serving as the Senior General Manager & CIO, Head of Digital & Technology at South Indian Bank. With a background in Computer Technology and Banking, Sony has held various managerial positions within the bank, focusing on digital banking. Sony has also been involved in the Indian Bank Blockchain Infrastructure Company as a Member of the Board of Directors. .

Additionally, Sony is a Certified Information System Auditor and has a strong educational background with an MBA in Computer Technology, a Certified Associate of Indian Institute of Bankers in Banking and Financial Support Services, and a Bachelor's degree in Physics. Currently, Sony is also the President of the ISACA COCHIN Chapter
