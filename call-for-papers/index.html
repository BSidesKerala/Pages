What do we need?

Topics which we are interested in!

We encourage people from all areas of security to consider submitting to the CFP/CFW.

We’re looking for talks and hands-on workshops that cover a wide range of security oriented topics including:

  • Software Security, DevSecOps
  • Hardware and Embedded Security
  • Offensive/Defensive security
  • Network Security
  • RFiD, Bluetooth, and NFC
  • Mobile Communications (GPRS/3G/HSDPA, etc.)
  • Privacy, Data Security
  • Malware and Exploitation/Reverse Engineering Techniques
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Data Forensics and Incident Response (DFIR)
  • Human Factors and Social Engineering
  • Security Operations
  • SCADA and Industrial Automation
  • Policy, Risk
  • AI and ML
  • Lessons learned
  • Something you have built
  • Something you want to share
  • Other cyber security community topics

CFP closes on: 31 December 2024

Speaker benefits:
If your talk/workshop gets selected, you will get full access to the conference.
Speaking at BSides Kerala is a great way to meet others interested in your topic and share ideas across.
BSides Kerala will promote your presentation, link to your social media, blog, or web pages you desire.
For speakers from overseas, we will take care of your accomadation and USD500 as honorarium.
For speakers from within the country, we will take care of your accomadation for 2 days.

BSides Kerala is all about building a vibrant cybersecurity community where learning, sharing, and connecting comes first.
