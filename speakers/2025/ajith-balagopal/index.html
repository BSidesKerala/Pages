BSides Kerala 2025 Speakers

Ajith Balagopal

Vice President Enterprise Services and Cyber security at Envestnet, Inc.

Panel discussion: The SaaS Conundrum, Managing Exposure in a 2025 Financial World

As organizations continue to embrace Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solutions for greater scalability and flexibility, managing financial and operational exposure has become increasingly complex. In 2025, the financial world faces new challenges as businesses navigate the evolving landscape of cloud-based tools, vendor relationships, and compliance requirements. This panel will explore the risks and opportunities of SaaS adoption, focusing on the financial implications of shifting from traditional software models to cloud-based platforms.

Ajith Balagopal

Ajith Balagopal is a seasoned IT and cybersecurity leader with over two decades of experience in managing security operations and DevOps. As Vice President of Cybersecurity at Envestnet, Inc., he specializes in securing enterprise infrastructures and transforming security teams.

Beyond cybersecurity, Ajith is passionate about mentoring security professionals, improving operational efficiency, and keeping up with the latest advancements in security technologies.
